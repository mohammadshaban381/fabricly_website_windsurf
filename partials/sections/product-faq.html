Questions

Find answers about ordering, customization, and shipping.

What is your minimum order quantity?

We support both growing and established brands. Minimum orders start from 20 units per style, with better pricing and production priority for larger volumes.

Can I request samples before bulk production?

Yes. We offer sample development so you can evaluate fabric, fit, and stitching before committing to full production.

What is the production timeline?

Standard production takes 5-10 days, depending on order size and complexity. Rush options are available when timelines are tight.

Do you offer full customization?

Yes. We handle custom patterns, sizing, labeling, embroidery, screen printing, and heat transfer based on your brand requirements.

Can you match specific fabrics and colors?

We work closely with you to source or match fabrics and colors as closely as possible, with digital approval samples before production.
