FAQs

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before starting your apparel project with Fabricly.

  • What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

    Our standard MOQ is 100 units per style/color, but we offer flexibility for first-time customers and pilot batches. Contact us with your specific requirements.

  • How long does production take?

    Lead time is typically 25–45 days depending on order size and complexity. Sampling takes 7–10 days.

  • Do you handle international shipping?

    Yes — we ship globally via sea and air freight, including export documentation, customs clearance, and door-to-door delivery options.

  • Can I get fabric samples before ordering?

    Absolutely. We provide fabric swatches and pre-production samples (PPS) for approval before bulk production begins.

  • What payment terms do you offer?

    Standard terms are 30% advance + 70% before shipment. Larger or recurring clients may qualify for L/C or 60-day terms.
