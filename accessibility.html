Accessible support for apparel sourcing and production inquiries

1. Our Commitment

Fabricly is committed to improving website accessibility so visitors can browse products, read manufacturing information, and contact us with fewer barriers.

2. Website Features

Current accessibility considerations include:

  • Responsive layouts for mobile, tablet, and desktop screens
  • Keyboard-accessible navigation and form controls
  • Readable headings, labels, and link text
  • Alternative text for important images where practical
  • Visible form validation messages for required fields

3. Known Limits

Some third-party services, embedded links, or external platforms such as WhatsApp, maps, social media, and form processing tools may have their own accessibility behavior outside our direct control.

4. Ongoing Improvements

We review pages as the website evolves and aim to improve contrast, structure, keyboard behavior, text clarity, and mobile usability over time.

5. Feedback

If you experience an accessibility issue on the Fabricly website, contact us at contact@fabricly.pk or message us on WhatsApp. Please include the page URL and a short description of the issue.
