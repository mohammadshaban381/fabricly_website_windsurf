Cookie practices for Fabricly website visitors

1. What Cookies Are

Cookies are small files stored on your device by a website. They help websites remember choices, support basic functionality, and understand how visitors use pages.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These support core website behavior, including saving your cookie consent choice in local storage under fabricly_cookie_consent.

Analytics Cookies

Analytics cookies may help us understand page traffic, popular sections, and website performance. These should only load after analytics consent is saved.

Marketing Cookies

Marketing cookies may support future ad measurement or Meta Pixel-style tracking. These should only load after marketing consent is saved.

3. Consent Choices

When you first visit the website, you can accept all cookies, reject optional cookies, or customize analytics and marketing choices. Essential cookies remain active because the website needs them to remember your preference.

4. Third-Party Services

Fabricly may use third-party services such as form processing, analytics, advertising platforms, maps, social media links, or WhatsApp. These services may use their own cookies or privacy practices when you interact with them.

5. Manage Cookies

You can update cookie preferences by clearing this site's saved browser data, then revisiting the website to see the cookie banner again. You can also block or delete cookies through your browser settings.

6. Contact

For questions about cookies or privacy, contact Fabricly at contact@fabricly.pk or message us on WhatsApp.
